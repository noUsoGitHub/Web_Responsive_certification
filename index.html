Littleroot

Welcome to Littleroot Town, a charming tourist destination that will transport you to a world full of adventure and friendship! Located in the lush tropical region of Hoenn, Littleroot Town offers a unique experience for nature lovers and Pokémon fans.

If you move to Littleroot, you must not forget to visit Professor Birch's Laboratory, a fascinating place where research and science come together to study Pokémon. Here, you can receive your first Pokémon companion and learn about the diversity of these fascinating creatures on an incredible journey.

Hoenn Awaits

Embark on a thrilling journey through the Hoenn region, a land of wonder and excitement. Discover the captivating beauty of its diverse landscapes, from lush forests to expansive oceans and towering mountains.

Uncover the secrets of ancient ruins and encounter legendary Pokémon that have shaped the region's history. Test your skills as a Pokémon Trainer in challenging Gym battles and compete in exhilarating Pokémon Contests.

With a vibrant community of Trainers ready to challenge you, and a wide array of Pokémon species to catch and train, Hoenn offers endless adventures and opportunities for friendship and growth. Are you ready to seize the excitement that Hoenn awaits?
